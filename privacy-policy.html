Privacy Policy

At Skinique Dermatology, we are committed to protecting your personal and medical information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Sections

1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Your Info 3. Medical Records & HIPAA 4. Information Sharing 5. Data Security 6. Your Rights 7. Contact Information

Quick Summary

We collect your name, contact details, medical history, treatment records, billing info, and basic technical data from website usage.

All Protected Health Information (PHI) is stored in encrypted EHR systems. Access is restricted to authorized clinical staff only.

We never sell or trade your data. Sharing is limited to care providers, billing, and legal requirements.

You can access, correct, or restrict use of your records. Opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

This notice describes how medical information about you may be used and disclosed and how you can get access to this information. Please review it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Data Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as data automatically collected when you interact with our clinical services and website.

1.1 Personal Identification

Name, date of birth, and contact details collected during registration, booking, or consultation intake.

1.2 Medical Information

Health history, treatment records, and clinical photographs taken for tracking your dermatological progress.

1.3 Payment & Technical Data

Billing address, insurance details, transaction history, IP addresses, browser types, and website usage metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized strictly to provide premium dermatological care and improve patient experiences.

2.1 Clinical Care

Scheduling and managing consultations, communicating treatment plans, post-care protocols, and appointment reminders.

2.2 Administrative Use

Processing payments, coordinating with insurance providers, and enhancing internal quality assurance protocols.

3. Medical Records & HIPAA

HIPAA Protected

Skinique Dermatology adheres strictly to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) regulations regarding Protected Health Information (PHI).

3.1 EHR Storage

Your clinical data, including photographs taken for treatment tracking, are stored in secure, encrypted electronic health record (EHR) systems. Access is restricted solely to authorized clinical staff directly involved in your care.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or medical information to third parties under any circumstances.

  • Trusted third-party providers (pharmacies, labs) essential to your care.
  • Billing and insurance processing purposes only.
  • When required by law or valid legal requests.

5. Data Security

We implement robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

5.1 Digital Infrastructure

Our systems utilize industry-standard encryption protocols, ensuring all data in transit and at rest is fully protected.

5.2 Physical Facilities

Strict access controls are enforced at all physical locations to ensure the integrity of your medical records.

6. Your Rights

Patient Rights

As a patient, you have specific rights regarding your personal and medical data under HIPAA and applicable privacy law.

  • The right to access and request copies of your medical records.
  • The right to request corrections to any inaccurate information.
  • The right to opt-out of non-essential clinical communications (e.g., newsletters).
  • The right to request restrictions on how we use or disclose your PHI.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your data, please contact our Privacy Officer directly.
